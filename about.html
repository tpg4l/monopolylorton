About The Event

17th Annual Monopoly Tournament & Food Drive to raise money for LCAC charity

Date: Saturday, February 21, 2026 from 9am-3pm

Location: Woodbridge Moose Lodge

Entry Deadline: Wednesday, February 18th.

NEW FOR 2026 – Anyone can stop by and donate food or money to roll the giant dice to win a prize. All rolls win small prize, doubles win medium prize, double six’s win $50

Summary of event

Participants pay entry fee-$40 for pre-register, $60 at door. Goal is 100 players. Three Rounds, Two 75-minute rounds, One 60-minute final round
